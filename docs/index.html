Spends — Privacy Policy

Effective 4 July 2026 · Last updated 4 July 2026

Spends is an offline-first personal expense tracker for Android. It has no account, no ads, and no analytics or tracking on your financial data. Your information stays on your device — and, only if you choose to turn it on, in your own Google Drive. We (the developer) never receive, store, or have access to your financial data or your messages.

In one line: Spends reads your bank's transaction SMS on your device only to help you log expenses in one tap. Nothing is uploaded to us. SMS access is optional — the app works fully with manual entry if you don't grant it.

1. Information Spends accesses — and why

SMS messages (optional)

With your explicit permission, Spends reads incoming and existing SMS messages on your device to detect bank and credit-card transaction alerts and pre-fill an expense for you to confirm. This parsing happens entirely on your phone. The content of your messages is never transmitted to us or to any third party. Spends does not use SMS data for advertising or marketing. You can use the entire app without granting SMS access (via manual entry), and you can revoke the permission at any time in Android Settings.

Transactions and financial information you enter or capture

The expenses, income, categories, accounts/cards, and notes you add — whether typed manually or captured from an SMS — are stored in a private database on your device.

Notifications

With your permission, Spends posts a notification when it detects a transaction so you can add it in one tap, and to deliver your daily recurring-entry reminder.

2. Optional Google Drive backup

If you choose to enable backup, Spends stores a copy of your app data in your own Google Drive, in a "Spends Backup" folder that the app creates. Spends uses the restricted drive.file scope, which means it can only ever access the files it created itself — it can never see the rest of your Drive. This backup belongs to you and lives in your Google account — the developer cannot see or access it. Data is transmitted over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection. You can delete the backup at any time from within Spends or from your Google Drive. Backup is off by default.

3. Data we collect and share

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal data, financial data, or messages with anyone. Spends contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs that collect your data. The only network connection the app makes is to your own Google Drive, and only if you enable backup.

4. Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you remove it. You can:

  • Delete individual entries in the app (with a Trash/undo step), or empty the Trash to remove them permanently;
  • Clear the app's data from Android Settings, or uninstall Spends, to remove all on-device data;
  • Delete your Google Drive backup from within Spends or directly in Google Drive.

6. Children

Spends is a general-purpose finance tool and is not directed to children under 13.

7. Security

Your data is stored in your device's private app storage. Backups, if enabled, are transferred to your Google Drive over an encrypted HTTPS connection. No financial data or message content is transmitted to the developer.
